Simplify your affiliate link management and tracking with Linkt, the ultimate WordPress plugin for creating, categorizing, and analyzing your website affiliate URLs.


== Description ==

[Linkt](https://linkt.com/go/website/) is a robust WordPress plugin designed to simplify the tracking and management of your affiliate links. This tool is perfect for anyone looking to organize and monitor not just affiliate links, but any URLs you want to track.

With Linkt, you can shorten URLs to make them more user-friendly and easier to share. The plugin also offers multi-level link categorization, allowing you to organize your links in a way that suits your needs. You can easily view detailed visit statistics displayed on an intuitive line graph, providing insights into how your links are performing.

Additionally, Linkt supports tracking multiple URLs using URL parameters, making it easy to monitor different campaigns or sources. Whether you're a blogger, marketer, or business owner, Linkt provides a complete solution for optimizing your link management and tracking processes.


**NOTE:** Linkt is NOT an affiliate program for your website; it's a tool designed for tracking and managing your links, whether they're from affiliate programs or other sources.

== Installation ==

= Requirements =

Linkt does not require any other plugins.


= Installation =

Installing Linkt can be done either by searching for "Linkt" via `Plugins > Add New` screen in your WordPress dashboard, or by using the following steps:

1. Download the plugin via WordPress.org
2. Upload the ZIP file through the `Plugins > Add New > Upload` screen in your WordPress dashboard
3. Activate the plugin through the `Plugins` menu in WordPress

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Will Linkt work with my theme?
Yes! Linkt should work with ALL themes!

= Where are the Linkt settings?
The Linkt plugin settings are found in the WordPress Dashboard under Dashboard -> Linkt -> Settings.

= Does Linkt replace Google Analytics?
Linkt is a powerful tool for managing and tracking your URLs, but it doesn't replace the comprehensive analytics provided by Google Analytics.

While Linkt offers URL tracking features and basic visit statistics, Google Analytics provides deeper insights into user behavior, traffic sources, and overall site performance. Use Linkt alongside Google Analytics to get the most complete view of your website's metrics.

= Is Linkt GDPR compliant?
Yes, Linkt is GDPR compliant. Linkt does not track any personal user data. It simply records that a visit has occurred without collecting any additional information about the visitors. This ensures that your use of Linkt respects privacy regulations and data protection laws.

= Is Linkt an affiliate program for my store?
Linkt is NOT an affiliate program for your website. It's a tool to help you track and manage your links from affiliate programs or other sources.

= Something doesn't look right or is slightly out in the design?
This could be from the theme you're using, or another plugin. Again, please contact us so we can post a fix or give you custom CSS to fix it.
